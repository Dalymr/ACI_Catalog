About This Project

This project is the result of an internship with Ooredoo Tunisia.

Ooredoo Tunisia is a leading telecommunications company dedicated to providing high-quality services and innovative solutions to its customers. This internship project is a part of our commitment to improving and enhancing our services.

What Our Platform Does

Our platform is designed to provide efficient management and retrieval of critical network data. It enables users to:

  • Retrieve information about endpoints, subnets, EPGs, tenants, bridgedomains, interfaces, and more.
  • Monitor and analyze network statistics and performance metrics.
  • Streamline network management tasks to improve operational efficiency.
  • Enhance data security and compliance through advanced access control measures.

We hope that this project will contribute to the continued success and growth of Ooredoo Tunisia, empowering its teams to make data-driven decisions and ensure the optimal performance of our network infrastructure.
